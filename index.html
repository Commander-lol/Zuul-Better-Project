Go Sunset

Yet another variant of the "Zuul Better" BlueJ example project, titled "Go Sunset". It is set in the small town of Portsmouth, England and tells the tale of a lone drunkard making his way home after a night on the town. In his stupor, he undergoes a series of dramatic hallucinations, involving leopards, wizards and the HMS Victory.

Gameplay

Go Sunset is a terminal based text-adventure. It includes the following features:

  • 15 Whole rooms packed full of character
  • 5 Characters packed full of rooms
  • Boats
  • Leopards
  • UNICORNS
